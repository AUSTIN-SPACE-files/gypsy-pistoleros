Biography

Gypsy Pistoleros are the world's only Glam Punk Goth 'n' Roll outlaws — a cult sonic explosion mixing swagger-drenched punk anthems, dark cabaret decadence, and apocalyptic energy. Renowned for explosive, theatrical live shows, the Pistoleros stand proudly outside every genre — a cult of outsiders keeping rock 'n' roll wild, dark and defiantly alive.

Following the success of Church of the Pistoleros — hailed as one of 2025's most fearless and eclectic rock albums — the Pistoleros have conjured something even bolder with Dark Faerie Tales: a dark fairytale for the damned, a love letter to the beautiful and broken, and a twisted hymn for the strange of heart. Born from the fires of their own mythology, the album drifts through haunted woods and neon dreams — where sleaze-drenched punk anthems meet gothic grandeur, and glam swagger burns with outlaw soul.

Once upon a twisted dusk, in a desert where gods had lost their names, stood the Church of the Pistoleros — built from shattered guitars, broken hearts, and outlaw fire. It burned… but from the ashes rose a new cult, born of chaos and dreaming in half-remembered hymns. Now, Gypsy Pistoleros sing under starlight in tangled woods where belief is rhythm, glitter, and spellwork — legends in apocalyptic fur, crowned in scars, howling at the dark.
