Your questions

Everything you need to know before you join.

How much does it cost?

£5 per month for The High Order, billed monthly. The Chosen is £2 per month. No annual contracts, no setup fees, no hidden charges. Cancel at any time from your account.

 What do I get immediately?

The moment you join, you get instant access to all existing High Order videos, posts, and audio going back to day one. You'll also receive a free download of the full Duende a Go Go Loco album as your initiation, plus a welcome message from the band.

 Can I cancel any time?

Yes. No contracts, no questions asked. Cancel from your account at any time and your access continues until the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again.

 How do I access the private group?

After joining, you'll receive an email with your Facebook group invite link within 24 hours. The private group is where the band post exclusive updates, answer questions directly, and share content you won't find anywhere else.

 Is my payment secure?

All payments are processed securely through Bandzoogle's payment system, powered by Stripe. It's the same platform used by millions of businesses worldwide. Your card details are never stored by us.
