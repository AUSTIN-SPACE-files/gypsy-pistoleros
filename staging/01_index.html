Who are Gypsy Pistoleros?

The world's only Glam Punk Goth 'n' Roll outlaws. Gypsy Pistoleros are a cult sonic explosion, mixing swagger-drenched punk anthems, dark cabaret decadence, and apocalyptic energy. Renowned for explosive, theatrical live shows, the Pistoleros stand proudly outside every genre — a cult of outsiders keeping rock 'n' roll wild, dark and defiantly alive.

Born from the fires of their own mythology, Dark Faerie Tales drifts through haunted woods and neon dreams — where sleaze-drenched punk anthems meet gothic grandeur, and glam swagger burns with outlaw soul.
